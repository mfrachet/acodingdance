What building professional frontend apps is all about?

When typing "becoming a frontend developer" on my search engine, I have tons of results suggesting to learn JavaScript, HTML, CSS, DOM manipulation, picking a framework, choosing REST or GraphQL, and tons of other things.

While these lists are accurate, I would like to bring more to the table and talk about what we concretely have to deal with when building professional frontend applications.

What is the goal of frontend developers?

The goal of frontend developers is to implement beautiful UIs and to provide a seamless, fast and reliable user experience to people using websites and applications.

  • Implementing beautiful UIs is about styling
  • Fast means performances + perception of being fast
  • Reliable (technically) means testing
  • Reliable (functionally) means E2E testing
  • User experience, accessibility, responsiveness
  • User experience, SEO

Frontend is about empathy, caring and perception.
